Buff MoMo

cooking time: 30minutes,

ingredients

  • flour about 1 kilograms
  • minced meat 1 kilograms
  • green onions(4 scallion) dry onion 500grams, corander 2 (scallion).
  • 200ml of cooking oil.
  • 50grams of garlic and ginger paste.
  • 1 packet of momo spice, salt 2 table spoon.
  • tomatoes 200 grams

instructions

  1. first of all prepare a soft dough of a flour and keep it inside the plastic bag, so it wouldn't dry off.
  2. fine cutting of green onion, dry oninon and coriander.
  3. prepare a bowl and put the minced meat and mix all the onion, coriander, momo spice, garlic and ginger paste.
  4. heat up the oil pour it in the minced meat bowl and stir it for 5 minutes and let keep it in cool place.
  5. start to make the dumpling
  6. boil the water in a steam pot and put all the momo inside the steamer and cook it for 10 minutes in a small fire.
  7. MOMO IS READY TO BE SERVED

prepare the sauce

  1. heat the pan and pour some oil and fry the ginger and onion and sitr until it become brown
  2. put the chopped tomatoes, 1 table spoon of salt, and mixed spices(momo spices) and cook it for 5 minutes.
  3. after its cooked let it cool down for 5 minutes, and mix it very well, we can add water if you dont like thick sauce
